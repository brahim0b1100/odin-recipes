Description:

Create a tantalizing chicken shawarma by marinating tender strips of boneless chicken in a flavorful blend of spices.

Cook the chicken until charred and cooked through. Spread a creamy shawarma sauce on warm pita bread,

add the chicken, and top with fresh veggies. Roll it up and savor the irresistible taste of Middle Eastern delight.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless chicken thighs, cut into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons tahini paste
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Pita bread or flatbreads
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Sliced red onions
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro
  • Optional: hot sauce or sumac for garnish

      Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

    • In a bowl, combine 1 pound of boneless chicken thighs (cut into thin strips) with 2 tablespoons of olive oil, 2 cloves of minced garlic,

      1 teaspoon of ground cumin, 1 teaspoon of ground paprika, 1 teaspoon of ground coriander, 1/2 teaspoon of ground turmeric, 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon,

      1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust to your spice preference), juice of 1 lemon, and salt and pepper to taste.

    • Mix everything together, ensuring the chicken is well coated in the marinade.
    • Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, allowing the flavors to infuse.

      Step 2: Prepare the Shawarma Sauce

    • In a separate bowl, combine 1/2 cup of Greek yogurt, 2 tablespoons of tahini paste

      juice of 1/2 lemon, 1 tablespoon of olive oil, 1 clove of minced garlic, 1/2 teaspoon

      of ground cumin, 1/2 teaspoon of ground paprika, a pinch of salt and pepper.

    • Whisk the ingredients together until smooth and creamy.
    • Set the sauce aside for later use.

      Step 3: Cook the Chicken

    • Heat a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat and drizzle with a bit of olive oil.

    • Add the marinated chicken strips to the hot pan and cook for about 6-8 minutes, or until

      they are cooked through and slightly charred.

    • Flip the chicken occasionally to ensure even cooking.

      Step 4: Assemble the Shawarma

    • Warm some pita bread or flatbreads in the oven or on a stovetop griddle.
    • Spread a generous amount of the prepared shawarma sauce onto the warmed bread.
    • Add the cooked chicken strips on top of the sauce.
    • Garnish with your choice of sliced tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, and fresh parsley or cilantro.
    • For an extra kick, you can also add a drizzle of hot sauce or a sprinkle of sumac.

      Step 4: Assemble the Shawarma

    • Fold the sides of the bread over the chicken and tightly roll it up, creating a wrap.
    • Cut the shawarma wrap in half, if desired, and serve immediately.

      • Enjoy your homemade chicken shawarma packed with flavorful spices, tender chicken,

      a tangy sauce. It's a true Middle Eastern delight!
